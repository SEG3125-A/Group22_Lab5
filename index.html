About us

We are a family of cyclists who decided to turn their passion into a lifestyle. When we first opened our little bike shop we had no idea how many people were in need of bike services. Now our team of bike experts have grown and we want to help our community be more active and healthy. We offer a range of bikes, accessories, and repair services. This is a place where fellow cyclists could gather, share stories, and embark on new adventures.
